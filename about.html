What is Hobuddy's Goal?
The goal of Hobuddy is to bring people together to try new hobbies and make buddies in the process! Trying new things can be difficult, but doing it with someone else can make it not as scary. Hobuddy also serves as a way for people to enjoy group activities without needing to be part of a larger organization or club, as that can be mentally taxing to some individuals.

 

Why was Hobuddy made?
Hobuddy was made to fulfill a niche in the social media scene of bringing smaller groups of people together, even just one or two people, in a way that doesn't force them to be a part of a larger community such as in social media platforms like Discord, Reddit, Facebook, or Twitter. Hobuddy focuses on trying to make connections between individuals, rather than trying to foster a larger community, although there is nothing wrong with that. We believe, though, that sometimes less is more, and being closer with one individual can be more rewarding than trying to fit into a crowd.
